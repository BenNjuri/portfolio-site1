Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At BrightSmile Dental Clinic, we collect information necessary to provide you with exceptional dental care. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, date of birth, contact details (phone number, email address, mailing address) provided when you book an appointment or register as a patient.
  • Medical & Dental Records: Treatment history, X-rays, dental impressions, diagnosis notes, medications, allergies, and relevant medical conditions shared during consultations.
  • Insurance & Payment Information: Dental insurance policy details, billing address, and payment card information processed securely through our PCI-compliant payment provider.
  • Website Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and interaction patterns collected automatically when you use our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Scheduling, managing, and delivering dental treatments and consultations.
  • Sending appointment confirmations, reminders, and follow-up care instructions.
  • Processing insurance claims and billing on your behalf.
  • Communicating important information about our services, office hours, or policy changes.
  • Improving our website, services, and overall patient experience.
  • Complying with healthcare regulations, legal obligations, and professional standards.

3. Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal and medical data, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted via our website.
  • Encrypted, access-controlled electronic health record (EHR) systems compliant with HIPAA regulations.
  • Regular security audits, staff training on data handling, and strict access controls limiting data visibility to authorized personnel only.
  • Secure physical storage for any paper-based records within our clinic.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your data only with:

  • Your dental insurance provider for claims processing, with your consent.
  • Specialist referral partners (e.g., oral surgeons, orthodontists) when treatment coordination is required.
  • Trusted service providers who assist in operating our website and business, bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Law enforcement or regulatory authorities when legally required to do so.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, review, and request copies of your personal and dental records.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Opt out of non-essential marketing communications at any time.
  • File a complaint with a data protection authority if you believe your rights have been violated.

2. Dental Services

BrightSmile Dental Clinic provides general dentistry, cosmetic dentistry, dental implants, orthodontics, root canal treatment, pediatric dentistry, and related dental care services. All treatments are performed by licensed dental professionals in accordance with applicable medical and dental regulations.

Treatment plans are developed on a per-patient basis following clinical examination. Outcomes may vary depending on individual health conditions and compliance with post-treatment instructions.

3. Appointments & Cancellations

We kindly request the following when scheduling appointments at our clinic:

  • Appointments can be scheduled online via our contact form, by phone, or in person.
  • Please provide at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule an appointment.
  • Repeated no-shows without prior notice may result in a cancellation fee to cover reserved chair time.
  • Emergency appointments are accommodated on a best-effort basis and may be subject to different scheduling terms.

4. Payment & Insurance

Payment for services is due at the time of treatment unless alternative arrangements have been agreed upon. We accept major credit/debit cards, cash, and approved dental insurance plans. Patients with insurance coverage are responsible for any co-pays, deductibles, or balances not covered by their plan. We will submit claims on your behalf where possible.

5. Patient Responsibilities

As a patient, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate and complete medical and dental history information.
  • Inform us promptly of any changes to your health, medications, or allergies.
  • Follow prescribed treatment plans, post-care instructions, and scheduled follow-ups.
  • Treat our staff with courtesy and respect at all times.

6. Limitation of Liability

While we strive to provide the highest standard of dental care, BrightSmile Dental Clinic shall not be held liable for complications arising from undisclosed medical conditions, non-compliance with post-treatment instructions, or outcomes that are inherently uncertain. Our liability is limited to the extent permitted by applicable law.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us understand how you interact with our site, remember your preferences, and improve your browsing experience. Cookies do not contain personal medical or dental information.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for core website functionality such as navigation, form submissions, and security features. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand visitor behavior, popular pages, and site performance. Data is aggregated and anonymized.

Functional Cookies

Remember your preferences (e.g., language, region) to provide a personalized experience on future visits.

Marketing Cookies

Used to deliver relevant information about our dental services. You can opt out of these at any time.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Please note that disabling essential cookies may impact the functionality of our website, including the ability to submit appointment request forms.

4. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our business practices. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
